Oman's Top Attractions

Explore breathtaking landmarks, historic wonders, and natural beauty.

Must-See Attractions

Discover unique spots that define the soul of Oman.

Nizwa Fort

Explore one of Oman’s most iconic forts, known for its impressive round tower, historical exhibits, and sweeping views of the ancient city of Nizwa.

Jebel Akhdar

Famed for its mild climate and terraced orchards, Jebel Akhdar offers serene mountain escapes and the breathtaking fragrance of rose gardens in bloom.

Wahiba Sands

An ocean of golden dunes where you can experience thrilling dune bashing, camel rides, and star-lit nights in Bedouin-style desert camps.

Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque

A masterpiece of Islamic architecture, featuring intricate mosaics, towering minarets, and one of the world’s largest hand-woven carpets.

Muttrah Souq

Wander through a maze of spice-scented alleys and shop for frankincense, silverware, and traditional Omani souvenirs in this historic marketplace.

Salalah in Khareef

During the Khareef monsoon, Salalah transforms into a lush green paradise with misty hills, waterfalls, and cool breezes — a rare tropical escape in the Gulf.

Travel Tips

Plan smart and travel safe in Oman

Respect Local Culture

Dress modestly, especially at religious sites, and be mindful of traditions.

Best Travel Time

October to April offers pleasant weather across the country.

Transportation

Car rentals are the best option for exploring remote areas and natural spots.

Currency

The Omani Rial (OMR) is the local currency. Credit cards are widely accepted.

Seasonal Highlights

Explore the best of Oman year-round

Winter (Dec - Feb)

Perfect for desert adventures in Wahiba Sands and cool hikes in the mountains.

Spring (Mar - May)

Witness blooming roses in Jebel Akhdar and enjoy coastal escapes in Sur.

Summer (Jun - Aug)

Experience the Khareef season in Salalah with misty hills and waterfalls.
